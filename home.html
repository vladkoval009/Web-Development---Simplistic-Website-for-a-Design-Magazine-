Introduction:

Design is defined as doing or a planning something with a particular purpose or aim in mind. On the other hand, the design is solving problem through different kind of iteration processes as well as forming certain valuable needs on its solution approach. On the one hand, the word design has many challengeable perceptional understandings through which it is dedicated to the context that it’s being used as a specific term. On a certain level the design is about giving forms to the artifacts that try to solve problems by using different processes and tools to achieve its goal. The usage of the word artifact to its broad sense would be much more easier to explain or we would say to describe the product which was intentionally created for enlarging into physical goods, services, graphics, organizations and processes. To the level in which we can perceive them as domains within which the design methods tend to be useful. The problem that design is trying to solve should be in attempt to societal needs.
